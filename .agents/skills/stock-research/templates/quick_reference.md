# Stock Research Quick Reference

## Common Search Patterns

### For US Stocks
- Company info: "[symbol] investor relations site:sec.gov"
- Financials: "[symbol] 10-K 2024" or "[symbol] annual report"
- News: "[company] earnings Q4 2024"
- Analysis: "[symbol] analyst ratings price target"

### For International Stocks
- Add market identifier: "[symbol].HK" for Hong Kong, "[symbol].L" for London
- Use local sources: HKEX announcements, LSE news

## Key Financial Ratios to Collect

1. **Valuation Ratios**
   - P/E Ratio (Price to Earnings)
   - P/B Ratio (Price to Book)
   - P/S Ratio (Price to Sales)
   - Dividend Yield

2. **Profitability Ratios**
   - ROE (Return on Equity)
   - ROA (Return on Assets)
   - Profit Margins (Gross, Operating, Net)

3. **Financial Health Ratios**
   - Debt/Equity Ratio
   - Current Ratio
   - Quick Ratio

4. **Growth Metrics**
   - Revenue Growth (YoY)
   - Earnings Growth (YoY)
   - Cash Flow Growth

## Sample Workflow: Analyzing AAPL

1. **Data Collection**
   - Search: "AAPL investor relations Apple"
   - Search: "AAPL financial statements 2024"
   - Search: "Apple earnings Q4 2024 news"
   - Search: "AAPL P/E ratio market cap"

2. **Analysis**
   - Evaluate financial health from collected data
   - Assess competitive position in smartphone market
   - Analyze services segment growth
   - Identify risks (competition, regulation)

3. **Report Generation**
   - Use template to structure findings
   - Provide clear recommendation with rationale
   - Include risk assessment and disclaimers

## Tips for Better Research

1. **Start with official sources**: Company investor relations, SEC filings
2. **Cross-verify key data**: Especially financial metrics
3. **Consider multiple timeframes**: Short, medium, and long-term perspectives
4. **Benchmark against peers**: Compare metrics to industry averages
5. **Update regularly**: Revisit analysis as new information emerges
